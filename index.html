Ready to embark on a coding adventure?

So, you're thinking of building something? This website was created to advise you on which first steps to take in your journey, depending on what exactly you want to build and how you want to build it. It's essentially like having an experienced mentor there with you—but this way, you can get to work by yourself.

Most of the articles and resources are intended for people looking to get their feet wet with a new kind of tech. If you're trying to learn quickly, you don't have time to waste—go ahead and jump in!
